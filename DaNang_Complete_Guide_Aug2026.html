Food Budget Tips
Eat well for less
🔍 Find the locals first
Tourist restaurants near old town Hoi An charge 3–5× local prices. Walk 2 streets back to where Vietnamese families eat — same dish, fraction of the cost.
🍺 Bia Hơi
World's cheapest fresh-brewed beer: ₱23–₱45 per glass (10,000–20,000 VND). Brewed daily, light and refreshing. Available everywhere along the Han River.
☕ Coffee rule
Never buy coffee at tourist cafés near Hoi An old town (₱135–₱180). Find a plastic-chair street cart (₱56–₱79). Exact same coffee at half the price.
